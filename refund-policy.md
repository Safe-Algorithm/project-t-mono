Cancellation & Refund Policy

This Cancellation & Refund Policy governs bookings made through the platform for trips and travel packages offered by independent trip providers.

The platform acts as a marketplace connecting customers with trip providers. Each booking is subject to the rules described below.

1. Trip Types

Trips offered on the platform fall into two categories:

1. Guided Trips

Guided trips are organized experiences where the trip provider manages logistics such as transportation, activities, or guided experiences. Customers attend the trip at a designated meeting point.

2. Self-Arranged Trip Packages

Self-arranged trip packages include travel components arranged by the provider such as flights, hotels, activity tickets, or other travel services.

Cancellation and refund rules differ depending on the trip type.

2. Cooling-Off Period

Customers may cancel a booking within 1 hour of completing payment and receive a full refund, provided that the trip’s registration deadline is more than 24 hours away.

This cooling-off period helps protect customers from accidental bookings.

Payment processing fees may be deducted where applicable.

3. Cancellation Policy – Guided Trips

Guided trips become confirmed immediately upon successful booking(no pending provider state).

Customers may cancel according to the following rules, calculated relative to the trip registration deadline, not the trip start time.

Cancellation Time	Refund Amount
More than 72 hours before registration deadline	100% refund
Between 12 and 72 hours before registration deadline	50% refund
Less than 12 hours before registration deadline	No refund

Additional conditions:

No refunds are issued once the registration deadline has passed.

No refunds are issued after the trip has started.

Customers who fail to attend the trip (“no-show”) are not eligible for refunds.

Some guided trips may be marked Non-Refundable. In such cases, cancellations are not eligible for refunds once the booking is completed.

4. Cancellation Policy – Self-Arranged Trip Packages

Self-arranged trip packages require provider confirmation before booking arrangements are finalized(requires provider to move the sate from pending to processing/confirmed).

Before Provider Confirmation

Customers may cancel at any time before the provider confirms the booking and receive a 100% refund, excluding non-refundable processing fees.

After Provider Confirmation

Once the provider confirms the booking and begins arranging travel components (such as flights or hotels), no refunds will be recived upon participant cancellation


Some packages may be explicitly marked Non-Refundable, in which case cancellations are not eligible for refunds after booking.

5. Provider Cancellations

If a trip provider cancels a booking for any reason, customers will receive a full refund of the amount paid.

6. Platform Intervention

The platform reserves the right to review and resolve disputes between customers and providers.

In exceptional situations, including but not limited to provider failure, safety concerns, or service issues, the platform may issue refunds at its discretion.

7. Force Majeure

Refund eligibility may be affected by circumstances outside the control of the platform or trip providers, including but not limited to:

Natural disasters

Government restrictions

Airline cancellations

Public safety issues

In such cases, refunds or travel credits may depend on the policies of the relevant suppliers.

8. Processing Fees

Payment processing fees charged by third-party payment processors may be non-refundable and may be deducted from the refunded amount where applicable.

9. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider and the customer’s bank but typically take 5–10 business days.

10. Contact

For questions regarding cancellations or refund requests, customers may contact support through the platform.
